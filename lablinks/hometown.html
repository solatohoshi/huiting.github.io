Welcome to Guangzhou!

City Information

  • Province: Guangdong province in southern China
  • Has a history of over 2,200 years
  • Population: approximately 65,594,622
  • Districts: Yuexiu, Tianhe, Haizhu, Baiyun

Guangzhou (pronounced /gwung-jo/) is the capital city of Guangdong Province in southeast China. A major South China Sea port, it is China's southern gateway to the world as well as a hub city of the Belt and Road Initiative.

Guangzhou is famous for its food and history. It's an inclusive city that attracts tourists from around the world.

Top 5 Scenic Spots in Guangzhou

  1. Chimelong Paradise
  2. Baiyun Mountain
  3. Sacred Heart Cathedral
  4. Chimelong International Circus
  5. Museum of the Western Han Dynasty Mausoleum of the Nanyue King
